Vegetarian Chow Mein

Indian veg chow mein is a satisfying, flavorful, and texturally delightful dish. It offers a combination of the familiar (noodles) with the vibrant elements of Indian-Chinese cuisine. It's the kind of dish that can be both comforting and exciting at the same time..

The recipie below is for a vibrant and flavorful stir-fry noodles with crisp vegetables.

Ingredients:

Noodles:

  • 200 grams Hakka noodles or chow mein noodles

Vegetables:

  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, julienned
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped

Sauces:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon green chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar (white or rice vinegar)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional, for balancing flavors)
  • Salt to taste

Other:

  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil (neutral flavored, like vegetable or canola)
  • Spring onion greens, chopped (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Noodles:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the noodles and cook according to package directions until al dente (slightly firm).
    • Drain the noodles, rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process, and toss them with a teaspoon of oil to prevent sticking.

  2. Prep the Vegetables:

    • While the noodles are cooking, slice the onion, julienne the carrots and bell peppers, shred the cabbage, and mince the garlic, ginger, and green chilies.

  3. Stir-Fry:

    • Heat a large wok or skillet over high heat. Add 2 tablespoons of oil.
    • Once the oil is hot, add the garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© for 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add the onions and stir-fry for a minute until slightly softened.
    • Add the carrots and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes on high heat, until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.
    • Push the vegetables to the sides of the pan and add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the center.
    • Add the shredded cabbage and stir-fry for another minute until slightly wilted.

  4. Combine and Season:

    • Add the cooked noodles to the wok and toss to combine with the vegetables.
    • Pour in the soy sauce, green chili sauce, vinegar, black pepper powder, and sugar (if using). Adjust salt to taste.
    • Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes on high heat, tossing continuously until the noodles are heated through and well coated with the sauce.

  5. Serve:

    • Garnish with chopped spring onion greens. Serve immediately while hot.
